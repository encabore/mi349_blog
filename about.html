About Me

I am a senior attending Michigan State University who will graduate with a major in mathematics and a minor in information technology next year in the spring. One of the few careers that I want to pursue is web development, which I have been dedicated to for several years. As a freelancer web developer, I have accomplished the following:

  • Built distinct websites for the Assembly of Filipino Catholics in Michigan and WKDT Radio of the United States Military Academy at West Point to improve audience engagement.
  • Collaborated with other students using WordPress and PHP to develop an independent web-based simulator.
  • Customized over one hundred unique cards with Adobe Photoshop and Microsoft Word for birthdays and graduation parties.
  • Redesigned over five school webpages entirely from HTML, CSS, and Javascript using Bootstrap and Skeleton frameworks to improve student navigation.
  • Held meetings with other established front-end developers and IT administrators to maintain and update website information.

I also play the piano for several choirs, a few of whom requested their own website (refer to above). Another hobby that I am interested is photography, and while I do not consider myself a professional photographer, many of my friends believe that I could be one. Having the opportunity to shoot beautiful, striking photos is primarily why I am interested in travel.
